Aatrox

Aatrox was once an Ascended being of Shurima, a noble warrior who fought against the Void. However, after centuries of war and corruption, he became known as a Darkin, tricked by mortal magic and sealed within his own blade. After centuries of imprisonment, he returned by consuming hosts and replacing their bodies with his own form, wandering the battlefield once more. But he soon realized he could never be truly free. Now, Aatrox seeks to destroy everything in existence, hoping to disappear along with the end of the world.

Lore

Once honored defenders of Shurima against the Void, Aatrox and his brethren would eventually become an even greater threat to Runeterra, and were defeated only by cunning mortal sorcery. But after centuries of imprisonment, Aatrox was the first to find freedom once more, corrupting and transforming those foolish enough to try and wield the magical weapon that contained his essence. Now, with stolen flesh, he walks Runeterra in a brutal approximation of his previous form, seeking an apocalyptic and long overdue vengeance.
